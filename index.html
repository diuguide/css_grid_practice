CSS Color Grid

To use:

1. Click Generate Boxes to create a grid of boxes with randomly generated colors.

2. Once grid is generated, click Change Colors to randomly generate different colors.

3. To change the shape to circles, click Circles.

4. To change the shape to sqaures, click Squares.

5. To animate the colors, click Animate Colors.

6. To stop this animation, click Stop Animation.
